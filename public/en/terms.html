Shurp Rules: TL;DR

Before you hit the road, remember:

  • Shurp is not a taxi. It's a platform where people share available seats.
  • Cost sharing. Trips are not a commercial service.
  • No fees. Shurp doesn't charge for using the platform.
  • Be responsible. If plans change — let others know (no ghosting).
  • Your car – Your rules. Agree on pets and luggage before the trip.

1. What is Shurp?

Shurp is a technology platform that connects private individuals (drivers and passengers) heading the same way to share trip costs.

Shurp does not provide transportation services, does not manage vehicles, does not employ drivers, and does not offer taxi or courier services.

When users agree on a trip, obligations arise directly between the driver and passenger. Shurp is not a party to this agreement and only provides a technical platform for communication and cost calculation.

2. Participant Requirements

To use the platform, you must be at least 18 years old.

  • Drivers: Valid driver's license, valid MTPL insurance, and vehicle in proper technical condition.
  • Passengers: Trips must be booked in your own name. You cannot reserve a seat for minors without an accompanying adult.

Shurp reserves the right to request additional information or suspend an account if there are doubts about compliance with these terms.

3. Cost Sharing Principle

Shurp is based on fair cost distribution.

  • Latvian regulations state that drivers cannot profit from trips.
  • The platform's algorithm sets the maximum cost coverage amount.
  • Users agree on payments between themselves.

Important: Shurp does not process payments, does not maintain financial accounts, and does not charge commission fees. Users are responsible for their own tax and financial obligations.

4. Safety and Liability

  • Private MTPL covers passengers if the trip is non-commercial.
  • The driver has the right to verify the passenger's identity before the trip.
  • Users are responsible for their own actions, safety, and belongings.

Liability Limitation:

Shurp is not responsible for traffic accidents, user actions, losses during trips, or disputes between users. The platform is provided "as-is".

To the extent permitted by law, Shurp's total liability in any case does not exceed 0 EUR, as the platform does not charge for services.

5. Account Suspension

Shurp reserves the right to suspend or delete a user account at any time if these terms, regulations, or other users' safety are violated.

6. Community Guidelines

  • If you're running late or can't make it — inform the other party.
  • Repeated no-shows (ghosting) may affect your rating or platform access.
  • For inappropriate behavior, use the "Report" function.
