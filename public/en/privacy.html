Your privacy isn’t a feature. It’s part of the architecture.

3. What Data Do We Process?

Identity Data

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Profile photo (if added)

Required so users know who they're riding with.

Verification Data

If you verify your identity (e.g., as a driver through CSDD):

  • we do not store your personal document numbers,
  • we only save an encrypted verification status.

Location Data (GPS)

To find fellow travelers on the same route, we need:

  • origin and destination information
  • route data

Precise coordinates are processed only as long as necessary for trip facilitation.

Location data is processed only upon user initiative (e.g., using the "Use my location" feature for route input or sharing location via an external messaging app). Shurp does not perform continuous or background location tracking.

Communication Data

  • Chat messages between users
  • Technical communication with support

Technical Data

  • Device type
  • App version
  • Access time

This data is used only for system security and stability.

4. How Do We Protect Your Location?

We've intentionally built multiple layers of protection.

  • Location Anonymization: We don't use your exact address in search processes.
  • Dynamic Offset: We don't use your exact address in the general search list. Your route is processed in anonymized regions, ensuring no one can trace your exact trip starting point.
  • Progressive Access: The exact meeting point becomes visible only after trip confirmation.
  • Private Routes: Your regular routes ("Magic Button") are not publicly visible until you confirm a trip.

5. Legal Basis for Data Processing

We process data based on:

  • Contract performance (organizing trips between users)
  • Legal obligation compliance
  • Legitimate interests (platform security)
  • User consent (e.g., location access)

6. How Long Do We Store Data?

We don't like digital dust.

  • Detailed trip coordinates are anonymized after 30 days.
  • Chat history is deleted 30 days after the trip ends.
  • Technical logs are stored for a limited time for security and system stability purposes.
  • If you delete your account, personal data is permanently removed from active systems, except where regulations require retention.

7. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We don't sell your personal data to advertisers.

The platform may include advertising or sponsored content. Such content is served based on non-identifiable criteria, such as general route parameters or regional data (e.g., showing a coffee shop ad to users traveling toward a specific city), without disclosing your personal identity or contact details to advertisers. This processing is conducted in accordance with GDPR principles and our legitimate interest in maintaining a commission-free platform for our users.

Data may be shared:

  • between driver and passenger (only what's necessary for the trip),
  • with technical service providers ensuring hosting, system maintenance, and security.
  • Data is stored within the European Union or in accordance with Google's international data protection mechanisms. Data processing agreements (DPA) are in place with all service providers.

8. Security

  • Data is encrypted during transmission and storage.
  • Sensitive information is not stored in plain text.
  • Data access is restricted and auditable.

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • access your data, correct inaccurate data, request data deletion,
  • restrict processing, object to processing,
  • receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes, we will notify you in the app or on the website.
