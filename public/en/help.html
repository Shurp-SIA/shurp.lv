How it works?

Ride with your pack

Shurp is a community where empty seats become shared trips — drivers and passengers simply split the cost of the road.

Real talk, real savings

We've ditched platform fees and commissions so every cent stays in your pocket. No middleman, no markup.

As easy as it looks

Search for your route, chat with your travel buddy, and settle the fuel contribution directly when you meet.

Why we don't handle payments

To keep Shurp free for everyone, we stay out of the middle. Settling costs directly with your driver avoids extra fees and keeps the experience peer-to-peer.

Shurp is a platform for cost-sharing, not a commercial transport service. All arrangements and payments are made solely between users.

Searching rides

The Classic (Single) Ride

One-on-one match — one driver, one route, one seat waiting for you.

Your Pack Ride

When you see a "stack" with multiple drivers, you have a pack of Shurpers. More options, faster match.

Many birds, one tap

Your request goes to the whole group at once. First come, first served — as soon as one driver accepts, the ride is yours and you can start chatting.

How to find your ride

  • Enter your origin and destination
  • Set your preferred departure time
  • Adjust the time window and detour radius
  • Tap Search — Shurp does the rest
Driving with Shurp

The Magic advantage

Let the "Magic button" handle your regular commute automatically. It puts your routine on the map so you can focus on the playlist while we find your pack.

Two ways to roll

Use Magic button for the daily grind, or set a Preset Ride for one-off trips where you have a specific seat to fill.

Total privacy

Your regular routes are for your eyes only — invisible to the public until you decide to accept a ride request.

Zero commitment drama

You only commit to a drive when you say yes to an offer. It's always your car, your rules.

Setting the price

Shurp calculates a fair cost-sharing amount based on fuel prices and route distance. You can adjust within the allowed range — drivers cannot charge more than actual costs.

Safety and trust

Real IDs, real people

Every driver is verified through Didit ID so you know you're riding with a verified member of the community.

Your data is a vault

We encrypt your profile and sensitive info because your privacy isn't up for debate. Sensitive data is never stored in plain text.

The "I'm almost there" link

Tap a button to send your live location to your pack on WhatsApp so they can watch you arrive in real-time.

Good vibes only

Our rating system keeps the community awesome — it's how we reward the legends and filter out the rest.

The No-Ghosting rule

Trust is a two-way street. Once a request is accepted, both sides are committed to a smooth trip. Frequent no-shows affect your rating and platform access.

Verification & Safety
How does driver verification work?
We use Didit ID — a secure identity verification service. Drivers complete a quick ID check and receive a verified badge. We don't store your document numbers, only an encrypted verification status.
Do passengers need to verify?
Passengers don't need ID verification to search and book rides. However, drivers may choose to verify your identity before the trip — that's their right.
What if I feel unsafe during a ride?
In an emergency, always contact local authorities first. After the trip, use the Report function in the app. We take all safety reports seriously.
Data privacy
What data does Shurp collect?
Only what's necessary: your name, phone number, profile photo (optional), and route data for matching. We never track your location continuously or in the background.
Can other users see my personal data?
Your public profile shows first name, photo, car info, and rating — nothing more. Last name and phone are only shared with your confirmed ride partner.
How do I delete my account?
Settings → Account → Delete account. Personal data is permanently removed from active systems immediately. See our Privacy Policy for the full retention schedule.
Is Shurp free to use?
Is there really no catch?
No catch, no commissions, no drama. Every cent of the fuel contribution stays between driver and passenger. We keep the lights on through community "Cloud Coffee" donations and some route-based signposts from places worth a visit.
Does Shurp take a cut from the ride?
Zero. Shurp doesn't process payments at all — all cost-sharing is settled directly between driver and passenger when you meet.
Car & Profile management
Can I add more than one car?
Absolutely. List multiple vehicles and pick the one you're rolling in for each trip. Your garage lives in profile settings and is a tap away in the Drive section.
Can I edit a ride after posting?
Yes, as long as there are no active bookings. Tap the ride and edit any time.
How do I change a booked ride?
Booked ride details are locked to avoid confusion. Write to your passenger(s) in chat and let them know directly.
How do I delete a booked ride?
Let your passengers know, remove them one by one via the app, then the ride is free to edit or delete.
Booking & Requests
What if I need to cancel?
Use the in-app chat to let your travel buddy know as soon as possible. Since drivers are already heading that way for their own commute, a cancellation isn't a disaster — but communication is key.
What if someone doesn't show up?
We call that "ghosting" — a major vibe-killer. Frequent no-shows tank your rating and we keep Shurp legend-only by rewarding people who actually show up.
How do I pay for the fuel contribution?
All cost-sharing happens directly between you and your travel buddy when you meet. See our Terms & Conditions for the full breakdown.
Ratings & Community
How does the rating system work?
After each completed trip, both driver and passenger can leave a rating. Ratings are averaged over time and visible on your public profile.
What happens if I get a bad rating?
A single bad rating won't sink you — they average out over time. Consistently low ratings may affect your ability to match with other users.
How do I report a bad experience?
Use the Report button on the user's profile or within the ride screen. All reports are reviewed by our team.
